DeTech is an award-winning fire safety startup focused on bridging the early-detection gap in vulnerable communities. Our flagship product, **Fire-Link Lite**, is a dual-alert system that combines heat-sensing hardware with real-time GPS mobile notifications.

---

## 🚀 The Vision

In many fire-prone areas, traditional smoke detectors fail due to environmental factors, and emergency responses are often delayed. DeTech solves this by:

- **Detecting Heat:** sensors trigger before flames spread.
- **Mesh Connectivity:** devices communicate even in offline environments.
- **Community GPS:** instant alerts sent to neighbors and local fire departments.
