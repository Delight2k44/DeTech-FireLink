## 🚀 About

DeTech is a innovative fire detection technology startup born at the Central University of Technology (CUT). Our flagship product, **Fire-Link Lite**, provides early fire detection for underserved communities using affordable, solar-powered heat sensors with real-time GPS alerting.

## 🌱 Mission

To bridge the fire detection gap in underserved areas with affordable, life-saving technology.

## 👁️ Vision

A world where fire disasters no longer claim lives or livelihoods through early detection.
