Who We Are

Engineering Safety for the Unprotected.

Founded at the Central University of Technology (CUT), DeTech was born from a simple but urgent question: "How do we stop fire disasters before they destroy lives?"

Delight Chetter and Kabelo Makhetha joined forces to create Fire-Link Lite. We aren't just building gadgets; we are solving global challenges. By centering our innovation around the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), we ensure our tech creates resilient cities and protects the most vulnerable among us.

Our Mission

To bridge the detection gap in underserved areas with affordable, life-saving technology.

Our Vision

A world where fire disasters no longer claim lives or livelihoods through early detection.
